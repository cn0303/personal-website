# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction

A **design-led, narrative-driven** personal site for Chandran (engineer +
researcher, embodied AI / robotics). The aesthetic resolved during intake to
**"editorial-technical"** — not loud/maximalist "bold," but *confident
editorial typography* (serif display × sans body × mono technical accents),
generous whitespace, a robotics/blueprint visual language, and **one memorable
dynamic moment**. Story leads; projects are evidence. Mobile is a first-class
target, not a desktop afterthought.

**Type palette:** Merriweather (serif display) × Satoshi (sans, via Fontshare)
× JetBrains Mono (technical labels/thesis). Owner-stated font preferences:
Merriweather + Satoshi.

**Fork RESOLVED (sketch 001):** **dark cinematic-technical** wins — near-black, faint blueprint grid, full-bleed media zone (eka), provigen-style technical panels, bold Satoshi headline × italic-Merriweather emphasis × mono labels. Light-editorial and manifesto-minimal rejected. The cursor-following robot gimmick was rejected and removed (too literal a copy + janky physics).
